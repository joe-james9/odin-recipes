Chicken Fajitas

Description

Ahh the classic chicken fajita, never gets old. I could eat chicken fajitas every day for the rest of my life and I would be happy. Anyway, this recipe is perfect to show off your cooking skills to your friends, family, or even a date ;). It's full of flavour and bound to fill all the bellies in the room.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Onion
  • Peppers
  • Salt, pepper, paprika, cayenne seasoning
  • Tortillas
  • Grated cheese
  • Your favourite sauce (mine is mayo)

Steps

  1. Dice your chicken breast.
  2. Season with salt,pepper,paprika and cayenne on all sides.
  3. Add to a pan with olive oil on medium heat.
  4. Chop your onion, peppers into small pieces.
  5. Add to a seperate pan with olive oil on medium heat.
  6. Stir the chicken and vegetables to ensure they get cooked evenly. Keep stirring every so often.
  7. Add your tortillas to the microwave, they should only take around 30 seconds per tortilla.
  8. Once everything is cooked, place everything on seperate plates.
  9. Add the grated cheese to a seperate dish.
  10. Let the feast begin! Add your favourite sauces to really make it your own.
