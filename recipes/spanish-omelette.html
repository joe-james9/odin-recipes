Spanish Omelette

Description

This recipe will blow your mind! The Spanish omelette is a renowned recipe that can be tailored to your favourite foods. You can throw whatever you want in there, this recipe is my recommendation. It includes peppers, onions, cheese and ham.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • A handful of grated cheese
  • 1 pepper
  • A handful of chopped onion
  • 3 slices of ham
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Crack and whisk the eggs into a bowl until mixed well.
  2. Chop the pepper and onion into small pieces.
  3. Tear the ham into small pieces.
  4. Add the egg mixture to a pan with a splash of olive oil on medium heat.
  5. Wait for the mixture to solidify, you want the omelette to have a decent structure so you can fold it over easily.
  6. Add the cheese, onion, pepper, and ham. Spread them evenly across the omelette.
  7. Fold the omelette over itself.(this may be difficult at first but with practice you'll get the hang of it!)
  8. Wait a couple minutes for the omelette to solidify
  9. Take the omelette out of the pan and onto your plate. It might help to use two spatulas to pick it up easier.
  10. Sprinkle some parmesan, salt, and pepper over the top of your omelette.
  11. Bon appetit!
