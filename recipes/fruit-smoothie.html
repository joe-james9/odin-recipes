Fruit Smoothie

Description

My fruit smoothie recipe is sure to give you a boost of energy in the morning, but you can drink it whenever you feel like it! This smoothie is perfect if you want to incorporate more fruit into your diet, it's practically a cheat code for having your 5 a day. It's perfect to take to work, or just before a workout. I drink one of these to start my day right.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Raspberries
  • Blueberries
  • Blackberries
  • Banana
  • Apple juice
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Chop the strawberries in half.
  2. Chop the banana into slices.
  3. Add the ingredients to your blender, you want to aim for about a quarter full of your blender so it doesn't struggle to mix.
  4. Add around 5/6 ice cubes depending on the size of your blender, if you can fit more in, go for it!
  5. Add enough apple juice to submerge all of the ingredients, including the ice cubes. Then add a little bit more.
  6. Blend for around 1 minute, make sure everything has mixed well.
  7. Voila!
  • TIP - if your smoothie isn't blending well, add more apple juice to the blender. When it's stopped obviously!
